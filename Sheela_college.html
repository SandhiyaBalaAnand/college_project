Pongal celebration

The students of Rajeswari College Celebrated Pongal in a grand manner on 09th January, 2023. The celebration of the harvest festival “Thamizhar Thirunaal” included cultural programmes like folk songs, karagaattam, etc.,. Each department students prepared their own delicious sweet pongal in earthen pots and offered their prayers to sun god..
